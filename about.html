About Me

Hi! My name is Rachel and I am a self-taught data analyst currently working on personal projects and continuing the learning. After graduating with a bachelor of science in economics, I have had many hands-on data analytics project experiences in interpreting data, and getting insights out of it.

I enjoy exploring data as I believe data can tell meaningful stories, which can help people to make better decisions. Data analysis is just like a treasure hunt, I enjoy interpreting the data and finding treasures (insights) in it.

I have recently completed the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate program to enhance my data analytic skills and techniques.
