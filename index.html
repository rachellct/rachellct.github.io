Hello, I'm Rachel

Data Analyst

What I do

"Data is the most valuable resource in the world."

I believe data can tell meaningful stories, from numbers to big pictures. As a data analyst, I aim to use data to guide people and help them make better decisions.

I am skilled in processing and analyzing data, using analysis tools such as SQL, R, and Python, and creating data visualizations using Tableau.

Data Cleaning

A clean dataset should be tidy, sorted, and filtered. I am trained in data cleaning and preparation turning a dirty dataset into a ready-to-analyze dataset.

Data Analysis

What could we discover inside this huge amount of numbers? Exploring the dataset allows me to analyze the data, to gain patterns, trends, results, and suggestions.

Data Visualize

A big picture could explain more than words. Interactive dashboards and reports could bring insights and make people understand the data easier and more interesting.
